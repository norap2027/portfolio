madvillain album redesign + cd packaging

  • Re-design of the MF Doom and Madlib album “Madvillainy” based on an original modular typeface. The typeface was designed using 4 individual modules which were constructed into individual letterforms. The typeface was utilized in re-designing one of the greatest hip-hop albums of all time, emphasizing the poetic nature of both components. The handwritten quality of the font is evocative of the way Doom may have written out his most clever lines.
