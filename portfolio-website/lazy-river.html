the lazy river booklet + poster set design

  • Poster and booklet design of “The Lazy River” by Zadie Smith. Through the use of sinister suburbia imagery drawing on “The Stepford Wives” film, the unsettling nature of the visuals communicates the text’s theme of the ignoring of societies' many woes.In three visually cohesive sets, the posters and booklets each draw a particular point out of the larger theme. The first set emphasizes anti-intelectualism, the second political posturing, and the third social media facade.
