type as image rosalía magazine design

  • Magazine design of "Rosalía and the Blurry Borders of What it Means to Be a Latin Artist" article by Justin Agrelo. The article explores the notion of who gets to be classified as a latin artist and the evolution of latin music’s classification in America. Inspired by chladni structures, the type as image gradually becomes more complex and sprawling, reflecting Rosalía’s global impact and complexity as an artist, as well as her inability to be neatly categorized. Cover images by Petra Collins for 032C magazine.
