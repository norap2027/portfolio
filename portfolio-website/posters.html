museum exhibit promotion poster

  • Poster design advertising the St. Louis Art Museum special exhibition The Culture: Hip Hop and Contemporary Art in the 21st Century. The exhibition examines the widespread impact of hip-hop in art, fashion, and culture at large, showcasing a wide variety of multi-disciplinary works. The exhibit’s themes of cultural dissemination and broad impact are communicated here through sprawling line structures that originate from a single nuclear element, and the gradual abstraction of these lines.

  • Corresponding motion component advertising the same exhibit for a mobile/instagram layout. Based on the corresponding poster the sprawling lines are translated to a pulsing textural element, matching the beat of the soundtrack (Public Enemy’s “Fight the Power”).

helen rodríguez trías poster design

  • Poster design introducing Dr. Helen Rodríguez Trías, a monumentally important figure in women’s reproductive health. As she was most active during the 1970s and 80s this design utliizes second wave feminism imagery, specifically the proliferation of pins during this era, to communicate her area of expertise.
