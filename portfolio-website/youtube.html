youtube thumbnail design

  • Thumbnail design for the youtube video “I Watched Every Cher Movie”. The use of five different Cher roles and the minimal bold text immediately communicates the videos’ topic and disrupts the usual maximalist thumbnail design present in a youtube home page.

    view count: 2.4k

  • Thumbnail design for the youtube video “how to erase a career: the forgotten legacy of Faye Dunaway”. Through the use of various tabloid headlines and the centering of Dunaway’s most iconic moments, the design immediately communicates the videos’ topic and previews the examination of her career rise and fall.

    view count: 70k

  • Thumbnail design for youtube video “Salma Hayek, Harvey Weinstein, and the Making of ‘Frida’”. The foregrounding of Hayek previews an emphasis on her account of the production, while the bold and glowing text grabs the viewer's attention.

    view count: 5.5k

  • Thumbnail design for youtube video “how one artist left everything he knew…and became an icon”. The image of the artist, Philip Guston, in black and white communicates the topic and the time period, while the bold text emphasizes his importance and grabs the viewer’s attention.

    view count: 22k
